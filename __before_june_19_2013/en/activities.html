Activities

Ex Nihilo Winery Tour

The Okanagan Valley is widely known for is its variety in high quality wines, ranging from refreshing whites and bold reds, to delicious ice wines. The CUMC 2012 Organizing committee is very excited to be working with Ex Nihilo Winery to give participants the opportunity to experience the unique taste of their wines.

We will be departing UBC’s Okanagan campus in the evening after a day of talks for the short drive north to Lake Country. Ex Nihilo will be giving us a private tour of their wine making facilities, art gallery, and tasting room. On the tour, participants will be able to take in the beautiful view of the Okanagan Lake, basking in the sunshine, while hearing about the history of Ex Nihilo and how it got its name. Six different wines will be tasted, including one ice wine, and the tour will close with a question and answer period. As a little extra memento, participants will receive an Ex Nihilo wine glass to take home.

The cost for this event will be $27.

Women in Math and Science (WIMS) Dinner

The CUMC 2012 is pleased to continue the tradition of offering a Women in Math and Science (WIMS) Dinner. The theme of this year’s dinner is “Breaking Down Barriers: Challenges Facing Female Mathematicians.” The evening will begin with a round table discussion, held in the Centre for Dialogue in the newly renovated Library at Okanagan College, where panelists from both industry and academia will have the chance to share their experiences and discuss a range of topics. Participants will have ample opportunity to join the conversation with their own questions as well. Afterwards, dinner with be served outside in the Courtyard in the beautiful Kelowna sunshine. During dinner, the participants and panelists will be encouraged to mingle and to continue their conversations.

This year’s organizing committee has decided to make the dinner for women and woman-identified participants only. We thank you in advance for respecting our plans.
